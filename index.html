Egypt

The Pyramids of Egypt are among the most iconic and enduring symbols of ancient civilization. Built as monumental tombs for the pharaohs, the most famous pyramids are located at Giza, near Cairo.

The Great Pyramid of Giza, constructed for Pharaoh Khufu, is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World and continues to awe visitors with its massive scale and precise engineering.
